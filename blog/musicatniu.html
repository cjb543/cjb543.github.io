Music at NIU: A 4-Year Retrospective

Marching Band

My first year at NIU was a period of immense growth, struggle, and change. Marching band made up a large part of the self-discovery process that I experienced as a freshman. There are too many great things to talk about, as well as some bad things. Overall, the marching program was incredibly beneficial to my student experience, even with all of its turbulent ups and downs.

Wind Ensemble & Wind Symphony

As a computer science major, having the privilege of playing in both Wind Symphony and Wind Ensemble during the same semester was a monumental undertaking.
