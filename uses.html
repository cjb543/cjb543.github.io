Uses

Below is a list of various tools and toys that I use in my day-to-day. I have kept them with me for a long time. I have had chances to replace them. I did not. What still serves has earned its place.

Music

I play on a hand-me-down Buffet Crampon E11 clarinet. It needs a restoration, but I've spent too long playing it to know anything else. I have no reason to get rid of it.

  • Ligature: Vandoren Optimum
  • Reeds: D'addario Reserve 3.5
  • Digital Piano: Casio S350

For any voice work, recordings, or meetings, I use a Shure Beta 57a.

Desk

My daily workspace is built around a HON worksurface and an Ignition chair. It has been enough. I have not gone looking for something else.

  • Light: Tertial
  • Audio Interface: Scarlett Solo
  • Phone: A202KC
  • Keyboard: F75

Current Software

I use a myriad of software. Some of it is open source, some not. I have used both without much thought beyond whether they do the job. I have never found much value in arguing over tools when there is work to be done.

  • OS: Mint / Debian / Windows 10
  • IDE: Vim + Tmux / VSCode
  • Images: ImageMagick / GIMP
  • Video: ffmpeg / Shotcut / Premiere Pro
  • Audio: Ableton, Audacity
  • Shell: Bash
