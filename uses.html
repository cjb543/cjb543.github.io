Uses

Below is a list of the various tools and toys I use in my day-to-day. I'm usually too stubborn to change my setup often.

Music

I play on a hand-me-down Buffet Crampon E11 clarinet. It needs a restoration, but I've spent too long playing it in its current state to feel like I need anything else.

  • Ligature: Vandoren Optimum
  • Reeds: D'addario Reserve 3.5
