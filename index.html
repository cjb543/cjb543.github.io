About

Software engineer and musician. Most of my work lives at the intersection of the two: oulipian constraint, generative notation, and tools that change how people play.

Clarinet player of over 10 years. Picked up saxophone and piano along the way. After finding my calling with computer science in my second semester of college, I realized that a fulfilling life would be one where I further the interconnection between music and technology.
